Welcome toRestoran

The SD Restuarent, at Gachibowli, Hyderabad, presents to you a lavish Goan house that transports you directly to the quaint streets of Panjim, with multi coloured homes depicting the Portuguese era, lush green surroundings, clay roofed gazebos, and tiny cobbled streets. Our open kitchen allows you to observe our Master Chefs craft their culinary presentations from flavours across the Globe for your culinary delight.

At the bar ‘The Tinto’, we shakeup cocktails and other beverages, while ‘The Margarita Lounge Bar’ transports you to the streets of Mexico with a hacienda theme and graffiti on the walls. Live music makes you shake a leg or two or just relax in the Central Courtyard, while you can also party to retro, commercial or house music on different days of the week with the DJ of the Night at the Lounge. To add up, our banqueting lawns have been created for lavish parties, reception or corporate events.
